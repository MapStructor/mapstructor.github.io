About MapStructor

MapStructor is a tool for making maps. Draw features, bring in your own data, style it however you like, attach information to it, and share it.

It grew out of the engine behind interactive history-museum maps and became something anyone can use — historical or present-day, simple or detailed.

Free to use. You only pay if you need a lot of storage. Open source: github.com/MapStructor.
